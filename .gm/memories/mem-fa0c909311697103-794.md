gm disciplines mechanics: disciplines are isolated knowledge stores at <project>/.gm/disciplines/<name>/{memories/, code-search/}, each with its own corpus+index. Memorize-time routing auto-creates a not-previously-known discipline store rather than mixing it into project memories. The @<name> sigil enforces strict cross-discipline read isolation. Disciplines are tracked (never gitignored, .gm/disciplines is excluded from ensureGitignored); derived per-discipline caches follow the general transient-derived-state rule. Harness + every spool verb propagate @<name> verbatim through the dispatch chain.
