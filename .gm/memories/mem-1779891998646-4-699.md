Auto-recall on turn entry mechanism (rs-plugkit): the instruction verb attaches an auto_recall pack {query, hits, fired_at, turn_entry:true} to its response on the FIRST dispatch after a >30s idle gap or session-start. Query is derived from .gm/last-prompt.txt / .gm/turn-state.json; hits are the top recall results plugkit pulled before serving the instruction. Wasm-side wasm_hooks::prompt_submit exports exist for legacy hook-host integration but the current spool watcher does NOT invoke them - orientation comes through the instruction verb response pack instead. Subsequent instruction dispatches in the same turn carry no auto_recall (or the same turn-start pack); do not re-trigger manually.
