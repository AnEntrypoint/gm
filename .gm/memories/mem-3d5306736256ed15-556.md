BROWSER WITNESS PATTERN (iter57): page.goto on large pages (e.g. paper.html) times out on the default 10s playwright nav timeout. Always use: await page.goto(url,{waitUntil:"commit",timeout:40000}); await page.waitForLoadState("domcontentloaded",{timeout:30000}); then page.evaluate. The default {waitUntil:"load"/"domcontentloaded"} with no explicit timeout fails on big DOM. Witnessed working browser-1351.
