Multi-repo audit sessions that push several rs-* siblings trigger the cascade repeatedly, so the local watcher goes version-stale mid-session multiple times; expected shape is: forced reboot fails once with 'wasm not installed' crash-loop when an old supervisor holds the slot -- recovery is kill the stale supervisor+watcher pids directly then one plain bun x gm-plugkit@latest spool; queued spool inputs survive the reboot and are served by the fresh watcher, so never re-write them blindly.
