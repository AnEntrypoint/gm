host_log in plugkit-wasm-wrapper.js parses `evt:` lines from wasm-side emit_event and ALSO writes them via logEvent to ~/.gm-log/<date>/plugkit.jsonl. Before this fix, wasm-side events (embed_init_ok, recall_score_unavailable, memorize_reject, table_dropped, discipline_sigil_ignored) only landed in stderr/.watcher.log — making gmsniff blind to them when the watcher was supervisor-spawned with stderr redirected elsewhere. gmsniff also now walks up from cwd to find .gm/exec-spool/ via findProjectRoot so it works from any subdir.
