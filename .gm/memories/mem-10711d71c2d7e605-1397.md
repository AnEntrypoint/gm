ROOT CAUSE: plugkit-wasm-wrapper.js scanStalledTurns() flagged any non-terminal turn idle>STALL_MS(300s) WITHOUT honoring busy_until, unlike supervisor.js:196 checkWatcherHealth which returns early when status.busy_until>now.
