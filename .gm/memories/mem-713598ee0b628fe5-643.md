plugkit-supervisor.js (ships in gm-starter/bin/, installed to ~/.claude/gm-tools/plugkit-supervisor.js) is the single sanctioned launcher for the wasm watcher. It monitors .gm/exec-spool/.status.json mtime, SIGTERMs the watcher child on stale heartbeat with .shutdown-reason.json writeback, respawns with exponential backoff (2s base, 30s cap), and writes .gm/exec-spool/.supervisor.pid as a single-instance guard. gm-starter/lib/skill-bootstrap.js::spawnPlugkitWatcher spawns the supervisor, never the watcher directly. ensureNextStepWiring runs on every bootstrap to seed .gm/next-step.md plus the @-include lines in CLAUDE.md and AGENTS.md.
